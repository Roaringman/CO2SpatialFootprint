About

This website was created by Christian Haack, Roar Engell and Martin Hilljegerdes during a group project in the lectures Webmapping and Multimedia Cartography in the Master Programme of Cartography at the Vienna University of Technology, July 2017.

It aims to provide information about the environmental impact of air travel by visualization and statistical comparison.

Data sources:

  • UN Data: Carbon dioxide emissions per capita
  • Carbonindependent.org: CO2 emissions per flight
  • SMALL-M: How much CO2 is released by Aeroplane?
  • tabula-Treedata
  • world-geojson
